## 💡 Key Insights From This Package

### Insight 1: Always Analyze WORST CASE
Not best case, not average case. Worst case gives the guarantee.

### Insight 2: Constants DON'T Matter for Growth
O(100n) and O(n) are both O(n). Growth rate matters, not multiplier.

### Insight 3: Lower Terms DON'T Matter
O(n² + n + 1) is just O(n²). Higher power dominates.

### Insight 4: Exponential Is UNUSABLE
O(2^n) for n=40 is ~1 trillion operations. IMPOSSIBLE in practice.

### Insight 5: Small Optimizations = HUGE Gains
Fibonacci: O(2^n) → O(n) = 853,162x faster for n=35!

### Insight 6: Loop Patterns Are PREDICTABLE
5 patterns cover 90% of real-world code.

### Insight 7: Practice Makes PERFECT
Analyzing complexity is a skill. Practice on real code.
